I am a human-computer interaction (HCI) researcher studying equity in online work at the University of Tokyo 東京大学 and Carnegie Mellon University

Research Agenda

How might we design more equitable
online workplaces?

Work is becoming increasingly online, remote, and distributed. These changes have the potential to reduce and create inequities in the workplace. My research investigates the design of new technologies that promote equity in pay and participation in online workplaces.

Gender Equity in Online Freelancing

In many parts of the world, women earn less on average compared to men. Through surveys and big data analyses, I showed that gender rate gaps continue to exist in online freelancing, despite more flexible work arrangements.

Work-Life Balance and Remote Work

With collaborators at UTokyo and NTT in Japan, I am running an online experiment to understand how moments of leaving video meetings early impact people's perceptions across Japanese and US cultures.

Online Feedback Exchange (OFE) and Career Development

OFE systems let people test concepts with millions of users before going to market. With Adobe, I applied design-based research methods to create CrowdFolio, a rapid crowdsourced feedback tool for freelance designers and their portfolios.

About Me

Hello! こんにちは。


I am a Postdoctoral Fellow at the interdisciplinary Tokyo College institute in the University of Tokyo, where I collaborate with Dr. Hideaki Kuzuoka in the Cyber Interface Lab and Dr. Naomi Yamashita at NTT. I am also a Visiting Researcher at Carnegie Mellon University's Human-Computer Interaction Institute (HCII) with Dr. Laura Dabbish, where I am leading a fully-remote team of eight researchers and designers. I earned my PhD in Technology and Social Behavior at Northwestern University under Dr. Elizabeth Gerber in the Delta Lab, and have industry experience leading UX Research teams at Facebook and Piktochart.

When I'm not working from home, you can find me exploring Tokyo, looking for a beach, or playing my ukulele. よろしくお願いいたします。
